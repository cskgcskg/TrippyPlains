Flight Modes

Your pace. Your rules.

Switch between modes at any time with a single key. No menus, no loading.

Angle Mode

Self-Levelling

The drone holds itself level when you release the sticks. Pick it up in minutes. Perfect for beginners exploring any of the 13 worlds.

Acro Mode

Full Manual

No self-level. The drone goes exactly where you point it and stays there. The real FPV experience. Use it as a muscle-memory trainer for the real thing.
