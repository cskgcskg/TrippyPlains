Privacy Policy

Trippy Plains — FPV Freestyle Simulator  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Trippy Plains is a single-player offline game. We do not collect, store, transmit or sell any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, no advertising networks and no third-party trackers of any kind.

What data the game stores

Trippy Plains saves a small amount of data locally on your device only, never on a server. This includes:

— Your in-game settings (graphics quality, controller calibration, camera angle, flight tuning)
— Your best lap times on race maps
— Your preferred drone craft

This data exists only on your computer. Uninstalling the game removes it. We cannot access it and it is never sent anywhere.

Steam

Trippy Plains is distributed through Steam. Steam is a platform operated by Valve Corporation. When you purchase and play games on Steam, Valve may collect certain data (your Steam account information, purchase history, playtime) in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not receive any personally identifiable information from Steam beyond what is necessary to verify ownership of the game.

Steam Cloud saves (if enabled) store your settings on Valve's servers, subject to Steam's Privacy Policy.

Steam Achievements and Leaderboards store your in-game progress on Valve's servers. This is standard Steam platform functionality.

Steam Input / Gamepads

Trippy Plains supports gamepads and joysticks. Controller input is processed locally on your device to operate the in-game drone. No controller data is transmitted externally.

Children

Trippy Plains contains no age-restricted content and is suitable for all ages. The game does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top of this page. As a single-player offline game with no data collection, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the Steam store page discussion board for Trippy Plains.
